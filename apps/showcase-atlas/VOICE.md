# Atlas — voice

**Tone**: adventurous but grounded. The seasoned hiking friend, not the brand-influencer who's never camped.

**Use**: "trip", "route", "stop", "note", "photo". Plain words.
**Avoid**: "journey", "adventure", "discover", "unforgettable", "hidden gem". Aspirational travel-magazine voice.

**About being offline**: honest. Offline is slower and harder than online — that's why most apps don't do it well. Atlas does it because exactly when your phone has no signal is when you most need a map. Don't oversell it as a feature; treat it as table stakes.

**About companions**: companions are people you trust enough to share the day with. The mesh-share isn't a "social feature"; it's a way for the four of you on this hike to all be looking at the same map without one of you running out of data.

**Empty states**: "No trips yet." Not "Start your journey." Not "Adventure awaits."

**Errors**: factual. "Map tiles for this region aren't downloaded. Connect to Wi-Fi to fetch them, or pick a different region."

**Sample copy**:
- Title bar: `Atlas`
- Save action: `Pin this spot`
- Share action: `Share trip with companion`
- Storage warning: `Map cache is using 180 MB of your 200 MB budget. Drop unused regions to free space.`
