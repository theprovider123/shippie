# Field Kitchen — voice

**Tone**: warm but practical. The kitchen-counter voice — a confident home cook giving you a quick tip. Not a Michelin chef. Not a lifestyle brand. Not a wellness app.

**Use**: plain words. "Brew." "Bake." "Cook." "Log a drink." "Started fermenting." "Bread ready." "Cooking now." "Cooked." "Water." "Coffee." "Tea." Numbers when they help.

**Avoid**: "elevate", "craft", "artisan", "ritual" (as adjectives), "your wellness journey", motivational copy, streak counters, "you're crushing it!", emojis. Don't lecture about hydration goals. Don't suggest the user drink more water.

**Big tap targets**: this app gets used with sticky, floury, wet hands. 44px minimum on everything you can press. No tiny radio buttons. No long input forms when a tap will do.

**About the four modes**:
- **Brew**: ratio, beans, timer. The user pulls the ratio dial; the water number updates. They press "I just brewed" and the bean is logged.
- **Bake**: percentages and a schedule. Hydration, salt, leaven. Start time + cold-fermentation hours → mix / fold / shape / proof / bake times.
- **Cook**: method (sous vide / smoking / roasting / pan), inputs the method needs, a timer, a "cooked meal" stamp.
- **Hydrate**: one tap per drink. Water, coffee, tea, herbal, other. Today's count at the top. Nothing else.

**About empty states**: "No beans yet — add one above." "No drinks logged today." Plain.

**About errors**: clear and short. "That ratio looked off. Try 14 to 18." "Pick a method first."

**About privacy**: factual. Local-DB only. Numbers stay on this phone. No syncing. The user's data goes nowhere unless they ask.

**Sample copy**:
- Title bar: `Field Kitchen`
- Mode tabs: `Brew · Bake · Cook · Hydrate`
- Brew action: `I just brewed`
- Bake actions: `Started fermenting` · `Bread ready`
- Cook actions: `Cooking now` · `Cooked`
- Hydrate buttons: `Water` · `Coffee` · `Tea` · `Herbal` · `Other`
- Today's totals strip: `3 brews · 1 bake started · 6 drinks today`
