# Care Log — voice

**Tone**: caregiver-respectful, operational. The voice of a competent steward who logs facts so the next caregiver has them — and so a doctor has them when needed.

**Use**: "Mum", "Dad", "the recipient" (when no name set), "gave", "noted", "handover". Plain stewardship.
**Avoid**: characterising the cared-for person ("Mum is being difficult", "Dad won't take his meds") — Care Log is logistics, not commentary. No surveillance framing ("Track every med they took!"). No emoji.

**About the recipient**: never characterised. They're "Mum" or whatever name the caregiver set. Their data; the caregivers are stewards. No personality assessment, no "compliance score", no judgment. The recipient's dignity is load-bearing.

**About other caregivers**: in paired mode, they're "the other caregiver" or "Sara" / "Tom" if a member name is set. Never blameful copy when they haven't synced.

**Empty states**: "No meds logged yet." Not "Welcome!" Not "Get started by adding a med."

**Errors**: useful. "The other caregiver hasn't synced in 6 hours." Never blameful.

**Sample copy**:
- Title bar: `Care Log`
- Add action: `Mark dose given`
- Symptom: `Mum reported headache 4/5 at 11am.`
- Handover: `Sara — GP appointment Friday at 2pm, fasting from midnight.`
- Privacy note: `Care records stay on the paired devices. Sync uses sealed relay traffic, so Shippie does not have readable access to what you write here.`
