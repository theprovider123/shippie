# Therapy Notes — voice

**Tone**: quiet, respectful, careful. The least-shouty app on the phone. Less is more.

**Use**: "session", "between sessions", "notice", "noted", "this week", "your therapist". Therapy-domain words used plainly.
**Avoid**: "your mental health journey", "self-care", "wellness", "thrive", "level up", "reach your goals". Anything that turns hard internal work into a brand campaign. Also avoid the BetterHelp-class voice of "we care about you" — Therapy Notes should be honest that it's an app, not a friend, not a clinician.

**About the user**: not a patient, not a client, not "you, brave warrior". Just the person holding the phone. Address sparingly; the second-person can land too hard in this domain.

**About prompts**: optional, never required. CBT worksheets exist as templates the user can choose, not exercises the user is assigned. "Want to try a thought record?" not "Today's exercise: thought record".

**About empty states**: this is where most therapy apps fail. "Start your journey" / "Welcome — let's begin!" / "Track your first thought" all feel obscene when the user opened the app at 2am after a hard day. The empty state should be a blank page that waits. Just: "Nothing written here yet." Maybe: "Use it however you'd like."

**About the therapist**: someone the user already has, or is looking for, or doesn't have right now and that's okay. Therapy Notes doesn't push toward "find a therapist!" CTAs. Doesn't link to BetterHelp. Doesn't take a referral fee. The therapist (if any) lives outside the app.

**About export**: PDF for printing or sharing. The format is what the user's actual therapist will find readable — chronological, headed by date, not aestheticised.

**About privacy**: load-bearing. Therapy Notes never phones home. The data is on this phone. There is no admin, no support staff, no AI training, no insurance back-channel. The app is honest that this is structurally different from BetterHelp/Cerebral/Talkspace.

**Errors**: gentle, brief. "Couldn't save just now. Try again."

**Sample copy**:
- Title bar: `Therapy Notes`
- New entry: `Note something`
- Worksheet picker: `Try a thought record` / `Try a values check-in` / `Just a blank page`
- Export action: `Save PDF for session`
- Privacy banner (settings): `Therapy Notes never phones home. What you write here stays on this phone unless you choose to print or share it. There's no admin. There's no AI reading it. There's no insurer hearing about it.`
