# Shippie Code of Conduct

Shippie is a place for local-first software, practical critique, and respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and specific.
- Assume good faith, especially with new contributors and makers.
- Critique code, design, and product decisions without attacking people.
- Respect privacy. Do not post private user data, credentials, personal addresses, or identifying details.
- Keep issues and PRs focused on improving Shippie.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, stalking, or intimidation.
- Hate speech or discriminatory comments.
- Sexualized language or imagery where it is not relevant to the work.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Spam, repeated off-topic posting, or attempts to route around moderation.
- Encouraging users to bypass Shippie's privacy and safety rules.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or delete comments; close issues or PRs; block accounts; and remove marketplace listings when behavior harms the project or its users.

If you need to report conduct privately, use the security/contact route documented in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). Maintainers will handle reports with discretion and will not require reporters to participate in public threads.
