# Parade Companion Launch Pack

Launch URL: https://shippie.app/run/parade-companion/

QR asset: `parade-companion-qr.svg`

Use this pack for the one-time parade launch. The promise is deliberately narrow:

- Open it on Wi-Fi before travelling.
- It saves the map, fonts, route pack, group plan, and safety info to the phone.
- It still works with no signal.
- It is unofficial and fan-made.
- It is for being at the parade, not for posting a public wall from the parade.

## Pre-Event Message

Phones may struggle at the parade. Save this before you go:

https://shippie.app/run/parade-companion/

Offline map, group plan, quick signals, bus sightings, and safety info. No account. Unofficial fan-made tool.

Open it once on Wi-Fi before you leave. When you tap Offline it should say:

> Saved offline
> Map and fonts are on this phone · pack 22 May 2026, 12:00

## QR Placement

Use the QR on:
