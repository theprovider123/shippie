# Parade Companion Launch Check

Date: 23 May 2026

## Launch Copy

Open this on Wi-Fi before you leave. It saves the parade map to your phone and still works if signal dies.

Use it for:

- Map: where you are, where the parade corridor is, and quick bus / crowd taps.
- Group: save your fallback meeting point and share it with friends.
- Safety: what to do if signal dies, the crowd stops, or you need help.
