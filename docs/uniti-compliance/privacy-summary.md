# Your data stays in your school cloud

A plain-English summary of how Uniti handles your school's information. For
staff, governors, and parents.

## The short version

**Shippie delivers the app. Your school cloud holds the data.**

When your school uses Uniti, you get your **own private space** in the cloud.
Everything about your pupils — class lists, lesson feedback, teacher notes,
suggested adaptations — lives **only in your school's space**. No other school
can see it. The company that makes the app (Shippie) keeps only basic details
needed to run your app (your school's name and settings) — **never your pupils'
information**.

## What we store, and where

- **Your private school space** holds: class and pupil lists, the quick feedback
  teachers tap after a lesson, optional notes, and the adaptations Uniti
  suggests. This is yours.
- **The app platform** holds only: your school name, your chosen settings, and
  who is allowed in. No pupil data.

## How AI is used (and how it's kept safe)

Uniti can suggest practical teaching adaptations using AI. Before anything is
sent to the AI:

1. We check the school has AI **switched on** (you control this).
2. We **remove any safeguarding or welfare information** entirely — it is never
   sent to AI.
3. We **replace pupils' names** with neutral labels (e.g. "Pupil A").
4. We send only the **minimum** needed, and we **record** every AI request.

AI only ever **suggests** — a teacher always decides. With AI switched off,
Uniti still works using built-in teaching strategies.

## Safeguarding notes are protected

If a note looks like it concerns a child's welfare or safety, Uniti
**automatically restricts it**: it is hidden from the normal feedback lists and
is **never** sent to AI. Only authorised staff can see it.

## Your rights and choices

- **See everything:** a school administrator can export your school's complete
  data at any time.
- **Delete a pupil's information:** on request, we remove that pupil's personal
  details while keeping only anonymous totals.
- **Delete everything:** a school can erase its entire space; we keep only a
  dated record proving the deletion happened.
- **Control how long we keep notes:** your school chooses how long detailed notes
  are kept; after that they're cleared automatically (totals are kept).

## Keeping it honest

Everything important that happens — who accessed what, every AI request, every
export or deletion — is **logged and visible** to your school's administrators on
the **Privacy & data** screen. Even our own support staff cannot quietly look at
your data: any such access is recorded and shown to you.

> Questions? Speak to your school's Data Protection Officer. The full legal
